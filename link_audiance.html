Audiance

The target audiance is young women, whose age is between 17~ 29. Since young girl often spent much of time browsing around the internet, and prefer online shopping, which is relatively convinient and do not have to face people in real life. Most of them are the university student, or at the early working age. On the other hand, there are a few male audiance, whose sexual preference are more of femininty, that may feel uncomfortable to shop in a cosmetic shop publically.

Therefore, Considering the target audiance, the design of the website have to be visually cute, pleasing and casual.
